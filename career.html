Current Role

Nordic Marketing Manager

Bethesda / Xbox Game Studios

2021 – Present

Overseeing Nordic marketing for some of gaming's most iconic franchises — Fallout, Starfield, The Elder Scrolls, Indiana Jones, DOOM, Wolfenstein and more. Responsible for regional strategy, campaign execution, media relations, and community engagement across the Nordic markets.

  • Led Nordic marketing for Deathloop PS5 launch
  • Managed campaigns for major DLC and seasonal updates
  • Coordinated with global marketing teams across Bethesda franchises
  • Built relationships with Nordic media and influencer networks

The journey so far.

  1. 2019 – 2021

    Thunderful

    Marketing Manager / Event & Influencer Manager

    Released 4 titles from Image & Form and Zoink under the Thunderful umbrella. Organized Thunderful's first ever Press Event. Attended PAX East, E3, TwitchCon Berlin, and Gamescom — managing press, influencer, and community outreach at each.

  2. 2017 – 2019

    Image & Form

    PR / Marketing

    Drove PR and marketing for the SteamWorld Dig 2 launch and led the studio's Gamescom presence, building media and community relationships that would carry forward into Thunderful.

  3. 2016 – 2017

    Tobii AB

    Marketing Coordinator

    Coordinated marketing for cutting-edge eye-tracking technology at Tobii. Gained broad B2B and tech-sector experience — and was featured on P3 Spel, Sweden's leading gaming radio programme.

  4. 2014 – 2016

    IHM Business School

    Education

    Graduated as a certified B2B IT Solutions Sales Representative — providing a strong foundation in solution selling, business development, and client relationship management.

  5. 2011 – 2015

    Community Roots

    Volunteer & Intern

    Online Community Manager for Retrospelsmässan, growing it to 3,000+ visitors. Volunteer moderator for EA Sweden. PR and social media intern at both Zoink Games and Image & Form. MSI technical support at Dreamhack Winter. Where it all began.
